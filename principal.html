A Tribute to Sister, Our Guiding Light

With wisdom, you lead us, steady and strong,
Guiding us forward, where we all belong.
Your vision, a beacon, so clear and bright,
Inspires us daily to reach new height.

Through every challenge, you've stood by our side,
With compassion and care, you guide with pride.
A leader, a mentor, a heart full of grace,
Your love for the school is seen in every face.

Your dedication is endless, your efforts profound,
In your guidance, we always have found
A sense of purpose, a path to follow,
A future that’s brighter, less hard to swallow.

As this class 12 batch, moves ahead,
Your legacy lives in them, by the words you've said.
We thank you deeply for all that you do,
The lessons you've taught, we’ll carry through.

In every moment of our youth, you’ve stood,
A beacon of strength, of wisdom, and good.
With gentle hands and a heart so wide,
You’ve built our dreams, been our guide.

Through every lesson, in every class,
Your wisdom has helped us to surpass.
A mentor, a friend, a source of light,
You’ve molded us, making futures bright.

In times of struggle, in moments of fear,
You were the voice that brought us near.
With patience and care, you’ve shaped our hearts,
Taught us that greatness comes in many parts.

Now as we stand on the brink of new days,
We owe it to you in countless ways.
For you’ve built the foundation, steady and true,
Sister, our gratitude will forever be with you.

Invitation to Our Esteemed Principal


Dear Principal,
St. Mary’s Convent School, Santragachi

It is with great respect and reverence that we, the students of Class 11, extend a formal invitation to you for the farewell event of the outgoing Class 12 batch.

This event is a momentous occasion for both the students and faculty, as we bid farewell to our seniors who have been an integral part of our school community. Your esteemed presence at this event would be an honor and would add great significance to the occasion.

The farewell will be held on 11th January 2025, in the School Auditorium. We sincerely hope you will grace us with your presence, offering your valuable guidance and blessings as we celebrate the remarkable journey of the Class 12 batch.

We eagerly look forward to your presence at this special event.

With the highest regard,
